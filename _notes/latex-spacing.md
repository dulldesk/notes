`\quad`  | space equal to the current font size (= 18 mu)
`\,` | 3/18 of `\quad` (= 3 mu)
`\:` | 4/18 of `\quad` (= 4 mu)
`\;` | 5/18 of `\quad` (= 5 mu)
`\!` | -3/18 of `\quad` (= -3 mu)
<code>\&nbsp;</code> | equivalent of space in normal text (e.g. to preserve it)
`\qquad` | 2x `\quad` (= 36 mu)
